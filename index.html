Ehtereal Chalet

A holiday in the heart of nature

Description

Located in Valea Mare Pravat, about 5km from Campulung Muscel, Ethereal Chalet is a special cottage that makes you feel closer to nature. Our goal was to create a space as welcoming as possible in which to spend quality time with family or friends.

The Space

The house is distributed on one floor, contains a large living room and a long hall from which you can access the 3 bedrooms and the bathroom. Bedroom 1 contains a 160x200 bed, bedroom 2 has a 140x200 bed and bedroom 3 has a 160x200 bed. All bedrooms have access to the back terrace. In addition to all this, the cottage offers a fully equipped kitchen with a table for 6 people, a special place for barbecue and much more.

Guest Access

Parking is free, it is in front of the gate and there is room for 3-4 cars.

To keep in mind

Guests can use the entire house except for a room that will be closed where we will store certain cleaning products and other consumables.
